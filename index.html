Our Mission

We are a group of alumni and current students from Lincoln Sudbury Regional High School (LS), Lincoln Public Schools and Sudbury Public Schools (SPS), seeking to hold these academic institutions accountable for vast racial injustices committed against students of color.

Our mission is to recognize and reconcile such injustices through: decolonizing of class curricula in all subjects, mandating certain courses and workshops for all years, beginning work in the K-8 system on racial injustices, ensuring concrete disciplinary steps in response to discrimination, acknowledging violence committed against indigenous communities, and recognizing injustices against METCO, Black, and non-Black students of color.

We hope that these actions will begin a long overdue reworking of our system. LS and SPS both pride themselves on having one of the best education systems in the state, but they also center white voices and continue to perpetuate racial injustice. Working with groups and individuals across the community, we hope to create a process of recognition and reconciliation that finally creates an equitable, fair, just, and anti-racist educational system.
